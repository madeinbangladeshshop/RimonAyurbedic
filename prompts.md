# Rimon Ayurvedic Banner Image AI Prompts (16:9 Ratio)

This document contains **5 professional banner image prompts** designed for the homepage slider or sections of the Rimon Ayurvedic storefront.

To accommodate text overlays and action buttons on the left, these prompts describe a **single continuous photograph with an asymmetrical off-center composition (rule of thirds)**. The main subjects are placed on the right, and the scene naturally extends to the left, fading into a soft-focus blur (shallow depth of field/bokeh) without any split-screen, diptych, or collage effects.

Please generate these images in a **16:9 widescreen aspect ratio** (using Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or FLUX). Convert them to **`.webp`** format, and place them inside the **`public/assets/images/banners`** folder.
